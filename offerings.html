Workshops

I create engaging sessions blending practical instruction with creative exploration for art/community/homeschool/writing groups, libraries, and schools. And, perhaps, you? Every workshop I run is:

  • Adaptable to: age groups, experience levels
  • Built around active participation with adaptive and fun exercises

I have a warm, generous, and enthusiastic presence. I'm fluent in English and intermediate in Spanish.

Types

  • Drawing For Fun: Accessible collaborative prompts, games, and excersises for enjoyement and expression, regardless of skill level.
  • Creative Writing: Targeted writing prompts to spark imagination, exploring narrative, character, and dialogue. Writing as nourishment.
  • Worldbuilding: Developing fictional settings through guided exercises, exploring techniques for creating believable worlds, avoiding common worldbuilding pitfalls.
  • Zine-Making: Learning multiple zine formats and binding techinques, creating a complete mini-zine during the workshop, resources for continuing their zine practice
