Pixel & Prose

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 6 Dec 2025 | Updated: 6 Dec 2025

Join me, Zachary Kai, for Pixel & Prose, a free online 'co-creating' session for folks who like making things on the internet!

Table Of Contents
  • How It Works
  • Previous Sessions

How It Works

This is a silent (chat-only, no audio) online session for working on your websites, programming, and of course, writing HTML! Come join us and get some focused creative work done alongside folks who enjoy doing the same thing.

It's hosted by me, Zachary Kai, as an offshoot of the HTML Day event I hosted.

When & Where

They're sporadic, but they're usually on Saturdays, always hosted on Google Meet, and no RSVPs required. To see the latest editions, see the Pixel & Prose category on the IndieWeb Events site.

Who Should Attend

This event series is ideal for anyone who enjoys creating websites and working with HTML. If you want a dedicated hour to work on your web projects in a supportive, peaceful environment, this is for you!

Important Notes

  • Silent Session: it's a chat-only event with no audio.
  • Technical Requirements: you'll need a decent internet connection.
  • Video: you're welcome to leave your camera on, but it's not required.
