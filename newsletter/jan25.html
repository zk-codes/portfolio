Herein you'll find an archived edition of the monthly newsletter: Jan 2025. Hope you enjoy!

Field Notes

What was supposed to be the January (and inaugural edition) is being published today! Please bear with me as I figure this out. If you have suggestions or advice, contact me!

Much achieved in and around work this month!

Writing and reading, lovely email exchanges, setting up my catalog, compiling stories for Echoes Of Distant Stars, preparing The Mara Files for re-publication, and sorting out the publishing process.

Digital cleaning and mending!

On the last day of 2024, goals for the upcoming year were posted. So far, two are done (setting up my store, launching the newsletter.) Onwards!
