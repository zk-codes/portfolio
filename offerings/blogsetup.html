Blog & Newsletter Setup

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 31 Jan 2026 | Updated: 31 Jan 2026

You want to write regularly and build an audience — but setting up the infrastructure feels like a whole project in itself. I handle the setup so you can focus on the writing. Whether it's a static blog, a newsletter, or both working together, I'll get it running, looking good, and easy for you to maintain.

What I Can Set Up

  • Static Blog: A clean, fast blog built with static HTML — no databases, no plugins, no fuss. You write, you publish. That's it.
  • Newsletter: Integration with a newsletter platform (like Buttondown or Substack) so your readers can subscribe and hear from you regularly.
  • Blog + Newsletter: Both working together — new posts go out on your blog and get sent to your subscribers automatically, or on your schedule.
  • Signup Page: A dedicated landing page for your newsletter, designed to match your existing site or stand on its own.

What I Work With

I adore working with:

  • Writers and poets building a readership
  • Creatives who want to share work and stay connected with their audience
  • People who already have a site and want to add a blog or newsletter to it
  • People starting from scratch who want something simple and lasting

My Process

Every project begins with you and what'll serve you best. I provide:

  • A conversation about what you want to write, how often, and who you're writing for
  • A recommendation on the best setup for your situation — not just the most complex one
  • Everything configured and tested before I hand it over
  • A short guide so you know how to post and manage things yourself going forward
