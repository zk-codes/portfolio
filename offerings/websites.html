Website Building

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 21 Jul 2025 | Updated: 11 Oct 2025

The internet gave us the gift of self-publishing. So why are so many writers and creatives still renting space on platforms they don't control? Algorithms, account suspensions, platform pivots...your work deserves a permanent home! I build static HTML websites: fast, secure, low-maintenance, and entirely yours. No WordPress headaches, monthly subscriptions, or tech complexity. Just a beautiful site that does exactly what you need.

Why Static HTML?

Static sites are just files, like documents! There's no database, no server-side code, nothing to hack or break. They load instantly and cost almost nothing to host. Many of the web's best personal sites are built this way.

I taught myself to code because I believe everyone deserves a space on the web. I've built and maintain my sites (zacharykai.net, lunaseeker.com, roadlessread.com) and I'm deeply involved in the indie web.

I believe your work shouldn't be held hostage. Nor should breaking free be hard.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I Update It Myself?

Yes! If you're comfortable editing HTML (or want to learn) you can update your site. The files are yours! If you'd rather not, I offer small updates for a modest fee, or we can discuss a simple ongoing arrangement.

What About A Blog?

For simple blogging, I can build a basic static blog or help you set up a complementary platform that links to your main site. Complex blogging is better served by dedicated tools.

What About Selling Things?

For e-commerce, I recommend dedicated platforms like Gumroad or Ko-Fi. Your site can link to these. Building a full shop would be a different (much larger) project.
