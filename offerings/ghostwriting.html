Ghostwriting

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 31 Jan 2026 | Updated: 31 Jan 2026

You have a story worth telling — but the writing isn't happening. Maybe you don't have the time, the confidence, or the energy to put it on the page. That's where I come in. I write it for you, in your voice, shaped by your vision. The words are yours to publish under your name.

What I Write

  • Fiction: Short stories, novellas, and novels — from your concept, outline, or rough notes.
  • Memoir & Personal Essay: Your experiences, shaped into compelling narrative. We work closely to get your voice right.
  • Non-Fiction: Essays, long-form articles, and book-length projects on topics you know well.
  • Blog & Newsletter Content: Ongoing written content that sounds like you, built around your ideas and themes.

What I Work With

I adore working with:

  • Literary fiction, speculative fiction, and experimental narratives
  • Personal essays, memoirs, and creative non-fiction
  • Zines, chapbooks, and independent publications

My Process

Every project begins with you and what'll serve you best. I provide:

  • An initial conversation to understand your story, your voice, and your goals
  • A sample chapter or section before we commit to the full project
  • Regular check-ins so the work stays on track and true to your vision
  • Revisions until the writing feels like yours
  • Full confidentiality — the work is yours, no questions asked

Pricing

Rates vary by project type and length:

  • Short Stories & Essays: $0.05 per word
  • Novellas & Long-Form Non-Fiction: $0.04 per word
  • Novels: $0.035 per word
  • Ongoing Blog/Newsletter Content: Monthly packages available — let's discuss

I also offer sliding scale rates. Let's discuss what works for you!
