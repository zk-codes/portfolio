Sensitivity Reading

Written By: Zachary Kai » Published: 31 Jan 2026 | Updated: 31 Jan 2026

Writing characters and experiences outside your own can be a minefield. Sensitivity reading helps you get it right — not by sanitising your work, but by flagging where representations might inadvertently cause harm, and offering ways to portray them with honesty and care.

What's Included

  • Inline Annotations: Notes throughout your manuscript highlighting moments that land well, moments that need attention, and specific suggestions for improvement.
  • Summary Report: A written overview of recurring patterns, areas of strength, and broader recommendations for the manuscript as a whole.
  • Context & Explanation: Where I flag something, I explain why — so you understand the reasoning, not just the correction.

Areas of Focus

I can read with particular attention to representations of:

  • Queer and trans identities
  • Disability and chronic illness
  • Mental health experiences
  • Cultural and ethnic backgrounds
  • Neurodivergence

If your manuscript involves areas outside this list, let's talk — I'll be upfront about whether I'm the right reader for your project.

What I Work With

I adore working with:

  • Literary fiction, speculative fiction, and experimental narratives
  • Personal essays, memoirs, and creative non-fiction
  • Zines, chapbooks, and independent publications

My Process

Every project begins with you and what'll serve you best. I provide:

  • A conversation upfront about which identities or experiences you want me to focus on
  • Feedback that's specific and actionable — not vague discomfort
  • Honesty: if something works well, I'll say so
  • A follow-up if you have questions about any of my notes

Pricing

Rates are based on word count:

  • Sensitivity Reading: $0.012 per word
  • Sensitivity Reading + Summary Report: $0.018 per word

I also offer sliding scale rates. Let's discuss what works for you!
