Lunaseeker Press is a queer independent press, publishing creative non-fiction, space fantasy, and zines run by Zachary Kai, an Australian artist.

Mini

Zachary Kai is a queer writer and generalist who crafts science fiction about resilient souls among stars in between making zines and collecting skills. Australian-born, he writes with American spelling and British punctuation: a linguistic wanderer. The internet is his livelihood and lifeline, and he documents his existence at zacharykai.net.

Short

Zachary Kai is a queer writer and avowed generalist who creates with unwavering purpose. Between crafting science fiction about resilient souls in the stars, making zines, and collecting skills like trading cards, he sends letters to strangers who often become friends.

Previously writing as Leo Flynn, he crafts tales with American spelling and British punctuation: a linguistic wanderer. The internet remains his livelihood and lifeline, and he documents his existence at zacharykai.net.

Medium

Zachary Kai is a queer writer and avowed generalist who creates with unwavering purpose. Between crafting science fiction about resilient souls in the stars, making zines, and collecting skills like trading cards, he sends letters to strangers who often become friends.

His work has been featured in the Bold Magazine, Dense Discovery, Stark Reflections, and The Rebel Author Podcast.

Though Australian by birth, he crafts tales with American spelling and British punctuation: a linguistic and world wanderer. He previously wrote as Leo Flynn. The internet remains his livelihood and lifeline, and he documents his existence at zacharykai.net.
