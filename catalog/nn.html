Nature Nurture

Published: 31 Mar 2024 | Updated: 31 Mar 2024

Nature, mother earth, the wild, call it what you like, but it's so always described in opposition to us. As though we're separate. Funny...

Folks love to philosophize and make their opinions known as much as everyone else, but language defines us and our ideas. Always.

It may be bold of me to suggest...but look around you. They say we have a crisis of being disconnected from nature. The way we see it hurts.

So, maybe it's that simple. If we saw ourselves as of and from nature. Maybe that's how we find our way home?
