Don't Burn Too Bright

In a galaxy still smoldering from wars past, Xander walks a tightrope between person and machine. Once a living weapon, he's now a fallen clockwork angel stitched back together from the ashes of his demise.

Everyone thinks he died three years ago, Gwen, his fierce partner-in-arms, and Isadora, whose introspective wisdom anchored his personhood.

Haunted by worlds they failed to save and life without him, Gwen and Isadora seek oblivion through the flickering Great Gates. Xander catches them just as they fall through, catapulting all three into an unknown future.

In a verdant city thrumming with energy, they face the chasm three years carved between them. Xander's unaffected exterior conceals a tortured soul, Isadora battles her demons, and Gwen struggles to reconcile the warrior she was with the person she longs to become.

Told through Isadora's poignant journal entries, this imaginative tale is for anyone caught between who they were and who they might become.

This isn't a story about the end. This is what happens after.

Echoes Of Distant Stars

From a deaf assassin navigating unfamiliar worlds to a gardener rebuilding civilization one seed at a time, each story in this collection is an intimate exploration of what it means to be alive in an impossibly vast universe.

Drawing on literary sensibilities and contemporary science fiction, these tales are portraits of resilience, redemption, and connection. Broken spaceships become sanctuaries, hostile planets offer second chances, and the stars themselves become characters in stories of survival and rebirth.

Perfect for fans of Ursula K. Le Guin and Becky Chambers, because even in space's furthest corners, the heart of the people continues to beat.

The Mara Files

Once the respected leader of the galaxy’s elite peacekeeping force, Mara Keres now rots in prison, betrayed by the one she trusted most.

She faces a critical choice: a dubious offer of escape or a life behind bars.

Before she can decide, her betrayer, Kottura, reveals her terrifying ambitions. With the galaxy in shambles, it needs a new iron-fisted leader. Mara is the perfect linchpin.

Hunted by mercenaries, and outsmarting Kottura's plots, Mara wants to ignore the galaxy's plight. But her freedom hinges on its liberation.

Will she reclaim her honor, or be consumed by the shadows she battles?
